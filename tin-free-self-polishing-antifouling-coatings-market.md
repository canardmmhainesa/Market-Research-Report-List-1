<p><p>Tin-free self polishing antifouling coatings are marine coatings designed to prevent the growth of marine organisms on the submerged surfaces of ships and boats. The self-polishing mechanism ensures a gradual release of the active ingredients as the vessel moves through water, maintaining long-lasting efficacy while minimizing environmental impact.</p><p>The tin-free self polishing antifouling coatings market is witnessing significant growth due to increasing environmental regulations and a shift towards sustainable marine operations. As more countries enforce stringent regulations on harmful substances in marine paints, the demand for eco-friendly alternatives is rising.
