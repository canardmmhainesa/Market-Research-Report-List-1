<p><ul><li>Electronic Flasher</li><li>Thermal Flasher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive flasher market is primarily divided into two types: electronic flashers and thermal flashers. Electronic flashers use solid-state components to regulate the blinking of turn signals and hazard lights, offering enhanced reliability and efficiency. In contrast, thermal flashers rely on a bi-metallic strip that heats up and cools down to create the flashing effect, making them simpler and typically less expensive. Both types serve essential roles in vehicle signaling but differ in technology and performance characteristics.</p></p>
