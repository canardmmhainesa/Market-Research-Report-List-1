<p><p>The fuel delivery system market encompasses several key components essential for optimizing engine performance. Fuel pumps transfer fuel from the tank to the engine, while injectors precisely deliver the fuel-air mixture for combustion. Pressure regulators maintain optimal fuel pressure, and filters ensure clean fuel supply. Fuel rails distribute fuel to multiple injectors. Additionally, air control valves manage airflow, and throttle position sensors monitor the throttle's position for accurate engine control. Together, these components enhance efficiency and reduce emissions.</p></p>
